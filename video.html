Spliting Trailer

This trailer showcases the gameplay and features of our latest retro-inspired game. Enjoy the pixel art, classic sound effects, and dynamic action as you dive into the world of Splitting!

Step-by-Step Tutorial: Creating the Spliting Trailer in DaVinci Resolve

  1. Import assets
    Four PNG images, one gameplay video, two music tracks, and titles were imported into DaVinci Resolve.
  2. Arrange timeline
    Images and video were placed in order to build the trailer’s flow.
  3. Add titles
    Text overlays were added to every image and over the video, each animated with fade-in and fade-out for smooth readability.
  4. Apply transitions
    Swipe left transitions were used between images for seamless visual movement.
  5. Work on audio
    Two music tracks were layered and volume adjusted, with fade-in and fade-out effects for natural audio transitions.
  6. Final touches
    Titles were synced with visuals, timing checked, transitions refined, and the finished trailer exported.

✅ Final Result: A polished trailer with smooth transitions, synced audio, and clear titles.
