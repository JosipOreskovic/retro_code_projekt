Spliting

  1. Setting:
    The bustling Mediterranean city of Split faces rising sea levels, unemployment, unaffordable housing, and an economy overly reliant on tourism.
  2. AI Governance:
    The city administration hands over control to an AI system to streamline governance and address these challenges.
  3. Your Role:
    You play as a recently dismissed professor, now working as a delivery person due to the AI's prioritization of other economic needs over education.
  4. Mission:
    Uncover ways to bridge the growing divide between the privileged city center residents and the marginalized platform dwellers.
  5. Development:
    Created by indie team: programmer Josip Orešković, composer Petra Žižak, and artist Matija Tomšić.
    Developed during Future Jam by the Croatian Game Dev Association and Austrian cultural forum at Aspira University of Applied Sciences in Split.

Step-by-Step Tutorial: Formatting Images in GIMP

  1. Open the image
    Launch GIMP and open the image to format (File > Open).
  2. Resize
    Use Image > Scale Image, enter the desired width and height, then click Scale.
  3. Crop for composition
    Select the Crop Tool from the toolbox, drag to select the area to keep, then press Enter.
  4. Adjust colors and contrast
    Enhance the image’s look with Colors > Brightness-Contrast or Colors > Levels.
  5. Apply effects
    Add filters or effects from the Filters menu to achieve the desired style (e.g., blur, sharpen, pixelize).
  6. Export the final image
    Go to File > Export As, choose PNG or JPG, and save the formatted image.

✅ Final Result: Images are sized, styled, and ready for use in the gallery!
